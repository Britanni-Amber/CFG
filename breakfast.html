Raspberry Coconut Chia Seed Pudding


Ingredients:

  • 2 cup unsweetened coconut milk
  • 1-1/2 cup raspberries frozen
  • 1 banana
  • 6 tbsp chia seeds

Directions

  1. Blend coconut milk, raspberries and banana together until smooth
  2. Divide chia seeds into 4 airtight containers like small mason jars (approx 1.5 tbsp each)
  3. Pour the liquid mixture evenly into each jar. Mix or shake until combined.
  4. Store in the fridge overnight and enjoy for up to 4 days.
  5. Option to layer with raspberries and top with granola!

Mint Choc-Chip protein shake


Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup spinach
  • 1 small banana- frozen
  • 1 cup unsweetened oat milk or almond milk
  • 3 mint leaves or 1 drop mint extract
  • 1 tsp mini dark chocolate chips
  • 1 scoop vanilla protein powder
  • Coconut or other non-dairy whip (for topping- optional)

Directions:

  1. Add all ingredients above to a blender. Blend on high until smooth, pour into a glass and garish with additional chocolate chips and coconut whip if desired!

Coconut Blueberry Protein Pancakes


Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 2tbs egg whites
  • 1/2c Vital Proteins Organic Grass-fed Whey protein
  • 1/4c almond milk

Directions:

  1. Mix all ingredients together in a blow until it’s the consistency of pancake batter
  2. Pour small amount onto skillet over medium heat
  3. Flip after about two minutes
  4. Top with fresh blueberries, unsweetened coconut flakes and 1tsp of organic grade A maple syrup.
