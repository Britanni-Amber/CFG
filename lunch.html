Healthy Fish Tacos


Ingredients:

  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 1/4 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/4 tsp onion powder
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 tsp freshly ground pepper
  • 1/2 tsp paprika powder
  • tbsp lime juice
  • 10 oz white fish (ex: (mahi mahi / cod) , boneless skinless chicken breast or even tofu
  • 1/2 avocado
  • 1/2 cup cabbage or radish to top
  • 2 tbsp 0% Greek Yogurt
  • 4 corn tortillas or lettuce to make it even healthier!

Directions

  1. Combined all seasoning together well
  2. Coat the fish in the lime juice and then add seasoning, rubbing to cover the meat.
  3. Preheat skillet or grill over medium. If using stovetop, add 1/4 tbsp of avocado oil to coat the pan.
  4. Cook for approximately 4 minutes on each side for fish or approximately 7 minutes per side for chicken (until cooked throughout).
  5. Warm tortillas or prepare lettuce “shells”
  6. Add the chicken or fish, cabbage, greek yogurt, avocado and fresh squeeze of lime!

Spinach and Strawberry Flatbread


Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup balsamic vinegar — plus 2 tablespoons, divided
  • 1 cooked boneless skinless chicken breast (roughly 8 oz)
  • 2 frozen cauliflower pizza crusts
  • Olive oil
  • 1 1/2 cups sliced strawberries
  • 1 avocado, cubed
  • 1/2 cup loosely packed fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • 1 cup spinach leaves, chopped
  • 3/4 cup crumbled goat cheese (yum!!)

Directions:

  1. To make a balsamic glaze for your flatbread, heat up 1/4 cup of the balsamic vinegar in a saucepan over medium heat. Then, simmer it until it cooks down gets a syrup-like consistency (you should end up with about a tablespoon). This takes about 8 to 10 minutes. Watch it closely and take it off the heat when it gets syrupy.
  2. Brush the cooked chicken with the remaining 2 tablespoons of balsamic vinegar, then cut it into strips or small pieces. Do whatever you like best on top of your pizza!
  3. Prep the crusts according to the package directions. You’ll probably need to cook them most of the way without toppings, but follow the instructions on the box! Basically, you’re going to brush the top with a little olive oil and cook until just hot.
  4. When it’s time to take the crusts out of the oven, get ready for the fun part: toppings! Arrange the strawberries, chicken, avocado, basil, spinach leaves, and goat cheese on your two crusts.
  5. Put the flatbreads back into the oven and broil them until they get a golden-brown look (so delicious!).
  6. Remove your flatbreads from the oven and drizzle them with your balsamic glaze for that extra touch! Cut them into strips, arrange them on a plate, and you’re ready to serve. Enjoy!

Chicken and avocado burrito


Ingredients

  • 4 burrito sized grain-free or sprouted grain tortillas, warmed
  • 1 pound chicken breasts, boneless, skinless
  • 1 large avocado, diced
  • 1 cup sharp raw chedder cheese
  • 1 jalapeno (optional)
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1/4 cup salsa verde salsa
  • Greek yoghurt, chopped cilantro and more salsa for topping (optional)

Directions:

  1. Place chicken, salsa verde, lime juice and chopped jalapeno in the instapot and pressure cook for 7-8 minutes
  2. Remove chiecken from instapot and shred it up
  3. Assemble burritos, top with your faves and enjoy!
