Oven Baked Salmon with Summer Salad


Ingredients:

  • 1-2 cups Mixed Greens
  • 1/2 cup Fruit (Berries, Citrus, Mango)
  • 2Tbs Olive Oil
  • 2Tbs Apple Cider Vinegar
  • 2Tbs Raw Honey
  • 1tsp Dijon Mustard
  • 2Tbs fresh lemon juice (or orange)
  • Pinch of Salt and Pepper

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Rinse and pat salmon dry
  3. Squeeze the juice of 1/2 of a lemon onto salmon in non-stick baking pan
  4. Season with pepper, a pinch of salt and parsley
  5. To make the dressing, Whisk all ingredients together
  6. Add the fruit to the mixed greens
  7. Use 1-2Tbs of the dressing and toss into salad mixture

Spicy Mexican Shrimp Bowl


Ingredients

  • 1 to 1/2 teaspoons chili powder
  • 1 to 1/2 teaspoons cumin
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 3/4 pounds shrimp, deveined & shelled
  • 1/2 tbs olive or coconut oil
  • 1/2 lime
  • 1-1/2 cups, cooked cauliflower or brown rice
  • 1 limes, juiced
  • 1 Tbs chopped cilantro
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • Fresh cilantro, cabbage, avocado, 0% plain Greek Yogurt, and salsa (option toppings)

Directions:

  1. Cook cauliflower or brown rice according to instructions. Over low heat, add lime juice, salt, and chopped cilantro. Stir for about 2 minutes then remove from heat.
  2. For the shrimp, mix all dry seasonings together in a bowl. Squeeze lime over shelled and deveined shrimp. Add oil to a skillet over medium and allow to heat for 1-2minutes. Toss shrimp in the seasonings and then add to the pan. Cook for 1-2 minutes on each side (3-5minutes total)
  3. Split the rice in two serving bowls. Top with shrimp and additional toppings of your choice.

Butternut Squash Chipotle Soup


Ingredients

  • 1 medium butternut squash, seeds removed and cubed (or pre-cut about 2-3 cups)
  • 1 box (15.2oz) of chicken stock, bone broth or vegetable stock (vegetarian version)
  • 1 Tbs chipotle peppers, canned in adobo sauce
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 Tbs olive oil
  • salt and pepper to taste

Directions:

  1. Pre-heat oven to 400 degrees
  2. Add one tablespoon of olive oil to a baking sheet and toss the cubed butternut squash, chopped onion and minced garlic.
  3. Place in the oven for approx 30min, checking and mixing after 15min.
  4. Add 1 table spoon chipotle peppers to a blender
  5. When cooked, add roasted vegetables to the blender along with the broth or stock
  6. Blend all together until smooth. Taste and add salt and pepper as desired
  7. Top with greek yogurt, pumpkin seeds and a drizzle of adobo sauce from can of chipotles
